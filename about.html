Our Story

A Vision Turned
Into Reality

EWoman Power Trading and Projects was established on October 2nd, 2018, by visionary entrepreneur Onalenna Caroline Johnny. As a 100% black female-owned company, we represent the spirit of entrepreneurship and empowerment in South Africa's fuel supply industry.

We are an independent and compliant fuel service provider, delivering petrol, diesel, and lubricants to various organisations and government departments across the nation. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted partner.

100% Black Female Ownership

We are proud to contribute to economic transformation and empowerment in South Africa. Our BBBEE status reflects our commitment to inclusive growth and the creation of genuine opportunity.

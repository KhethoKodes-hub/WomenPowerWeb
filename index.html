About Us

EWoman Power Trading and Projects was established on October 2nd, 2018, by Onalenna Caroline Johnny. We are a 100% black female-owned company dedicated to excellence in fuel supply services.

Our Mission: We maintain high levels of customer satisfaction while forming sound relationships for the benefit of all parties. We contribute to employment growth in South Africa and establish successful partnerships with our clients.

Our Vision: To provide high-quality service of choice that is reliable and distinctly different from competitors. We strive to be recognised as the best in all industries and sectors we operate in while pursuing excellence.
